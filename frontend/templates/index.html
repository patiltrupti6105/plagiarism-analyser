How It Works

01

TF-IDF Cosine Similarity

Converts documents into weighted term vectors and measures angle between them. Effective at detecting paraphrased content and topic overlap.

02

Jaccard Shingling

Generates overlapping 5-word n-gram fingerprints from both documents. Jaccard coefficient reveals exact and near-exact phrase copying.

03

Sentence Fingerprinting

Every source sentence is compared against all reference sentences using TF-IDF. Sentences above 65% match threshold are flagged and highlighted.

04

Blended Scoring

A weighted combination of all three algorithms produces a calibrated final score. Reports include per-reference breakdowns for transparency.
